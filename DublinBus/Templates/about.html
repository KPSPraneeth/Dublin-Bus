WELCOME TO THE TRAVEL HUB

The all-in-one bus travel directions and tourist information platform.

Here we will go through how to use each feature of our application.

Move the start (green) and end markers (red) to get a bus route instantly

After a journey is routed, the two closest Dublin bikes stations are displayed, displaying the station's name, total stands, available bikes and parking slots.

Use the search location bar to input start and end locations manually.

Searching is restricted to the Dublin area!

Be sure to pick your routing preference!

To see a step-by-step breakdown of the directions and more information about the journey ahead, hit the see full details button.

We can then see the details of the route ahead, which shows total travel distance, estimated travel time and how much the fare is going to cost in both cash and leap cards.
The estimated travel time is calculated using our support vector machine, machine learning models.

Below this, we display whether or not there is going to be a delay using secondary classification machine learning models.

Then you can view the directions tab, which displays step by step directions of the route ahead!

Explore our range of tourist attractions below. When a group or category is chosen, markers will appear on the map above their location.

Click on any marker to see a picture, rating website link and also directly get directions!
